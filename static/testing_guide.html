Understanding the Prediction Model

The model predicts whether a customer will be interested in vehicle insurance based on patterns learned from training data. The two most important factors are:

  • Previously Insured: Customers NOT previously insured are more likely to buy
  • Vehicle Damage: Customers with damaged vehicles are more likely to buy

Quick Test Pattern

To see a POSITIVE prediction, try this combination:

  1. Set Previously Insured = No
  2. Set Vehicle Damage = Yes
  3. Set Vehicle Age = > 2 Years
  4. Keep other values at defaults

To see a NEGATIVE prediction:

  1. Set Previously Insured = Yes
  2. Set Vehicle Damage = No
  3. Keep other values at defaults
